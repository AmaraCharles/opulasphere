FAQs

What is OpulaSphere?

A: OpulaSphere is an exclusive online marketplace that merges the worlds of Web2 and Web3, providing a luxurious platform for buying and selling high-end items like jewelry, accessories, bags, artworks, and other crafts. Our marketplace also features NFT collections, which additionally serve as certificates of authenticity for the physical items, offering a unique and innovative experience for collectors and sellers alike.
How does OpulaSphere work?

A: OpulaSphere allows sellers to list luxury items and NFT collections. Collectors can browse, purchase, and trade these exclusive goods. Sellers must verify their Ethereum wallets to access full platform features, ensuring a secure and trusted environment. Both physical and digital items are available, seamlessly bridging traditional and blockchain-based marketplaces.
How do I create an account on OpulaSphere?

A: To create an account:
  1. Create a digital wallet (a noncustodial wallet like MetaMask or Trust Wallet).
  2. Visit our registration page and provide your first name, last name, email, username, and password. Optional fields include phone number.
    3. Follow the on-screen prompts to complete your registration.

What happens if my Ethereum wallet balance falls below 0.5 ETH?

A: If your balance drops below 0.5 ETH, you will still be able to log in and browse the marketplace. However, you will not be able to earn on OpulaSphere until your balance meets the minimum requirement again.
How do I list an item for sale?

A: As a verified seller, all you have to do is upload clear images of your items, fill in all necessary details as you’re prompted to, after which the platform handles the minting process as well as listing.
What is the fee structure for sellers?

A: Sellers receive 15% of sales proceeds from their NFT collections while they’re allowed to keep 100% of sales proceeds from their physical items. This percentage can be adjusted in the future as the platform evolves. Additional details about fees and potential changes will be communicated to all users.
What are NFTs and how do they work on OpulaSphere?

A: NFTs (Non-Fungible Tokens) are unique digital assets that represent ownership of specific items or content. On OpulaSphere, sellers are helped to create and list NFTs as part of their collections. Buyers can purchase these NFTs, and sellers earn a percentage of the sales.
How do I verify my Ethereum wallet for NFT transactions?

A: During registration, you will be prompted to verify your ethereum address. Ensure your wallet has a minimum balance of 0.5 ETH for full verification. This process secures your transactions and allows you to participate in both physical and digital marketplaces.
How does OpulaSphere ensure the security of transactions?

A: OpulaSphere uses blockchain technology to secure all transactions on the platform. This ensures transparency, immutability, and trust in the marketplace. Additionally, we use advanced encryption methods to protect user data and financial information.
